# Important Limits Before You Order

There are two parts that matter more than the referral link itself.

## 1) Local market rules

Tesla says the program and its benefits are subject to the local laws and regulations of the registration address of both the referrer and the buyer.

That means a referral link may be active on one country's Tesla page, while a different country may have different terms, different qualifying products or no active buyer benefit at all.

## 2) Tesla's program intent

Tesla's official terms also say the program is intended for sharing with friends and family, and that paid advertising, selling or offering incentives for referral-link use is not appropriate.

So this page should be read as an information guide, not as a promise that Tesla will honor every referral scenario.

# Best Way to Check Before Ordering

If you are in a market where Tesla still runs `Refer and Earn`, this is the lowest-risk flow:

1. Open your local Tesla support page for `Refer and Earn`.
2. Open the referral link: [https://ts.la/woojin303338](https://ts.la/woojin303338)
3. Confirm that the landing page shows a benefit for your market.
4. Confirm that the order page still shows the referral benefit before paying.
5. Do not place the order first and try to apply the link later, because Tesla says referral links cannot be applied after the order is placed.

# What This Means for International Buyers

If you live in a country where Tesla's referral program is still active, there is a reasonable chance that the link will still lead you into a valid referral flow.

But I would not describe it as "guaranteed" for every country or every account combination, because Tesla's own terms make the outcome depend on market rules and account status.

In other words:

- `Confirmed`: the referral link is live and some non-Korean markets still have active official referral pages.

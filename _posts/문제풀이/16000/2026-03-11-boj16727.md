# 풀이

## 문제

The Iranian ChamPions Cup (ICPC), the most prestigious football league in Iran, is reaching its end, and people are eagerly waiting for the finals, which happened to be between the two most popular Iranian teams, Persepolis and Esteghlal.

The ICPC finals consist of two matches, with each team competing as the home team in one match. The winning team is determined by aggregate score, the sum of the scores of the two matches. ...

**입력 요약**  
The first line of the input contains two space-separated integers p1 and s1, where p1 and s1 are the number of goals scored by Persepolis and Esteghlal, respectively, in the first match in which Persepolis is the home team. ...

**출력 요약**  
In the output, print the name of the winning team, either Persepolis or Esteghlal, if the winner can be determined by the aggregate scores and the away goals rule. Otherwise, print Penalty.

## 코드

{% raw %}
```python
a,b=map(int,input().split())
c,d=map(int,input().split())
if a+d==b+c:
    if b>d:print('Esteghlal')
    elif b<d:print('Persepolis')
    else:print('Penalty')
elif a+d<b+c:print('Esteghlal')
else:print('Persepolis')
```
